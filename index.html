FAQ

Q] Does this kernel root your device?

- This kernel does not root your device. But if you were already rooted before installing this kernel, you don't have to root again as it preserves root.

Q] Do i need to wipe/format after installing?

- You don't have to wipe/format anything after installing the kernel unless you face any encryption errors on boot

Q] Is this kernel compatible with XX rom?

- Supported ROMs are already mentioned in the XDA thread

Q] Does this kernel work with XX model?

- Supported models are already mentioned in the XDA thread

Q] I have found a bug. What should I do?

- Just mention the details of the bug along with your device and rom name in OPTI-KERNEL Community group on Telegram
